Machu Picchu, Peru

About Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru on a mountain ridge. It is often referred to as the "Lost City of the Incas" and is one of the most iconic travel destinations in the world.
